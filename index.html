Update 2.0 - July 6, 2026

  • Added new feature: Unlockable characters! These are chosen before the start of the game and can be unlocked though progression. Currently, there are three new characters: Tank, Healer, and Mage. Each character has their own unique strengths and weaknesses.
  • Added 1 new tier 3 enemy
  • Balance Changes:
    • Increased time between attacks of arena closer (0.33s -> 0.5s)
      • Developer's note: Arena closer was too good at its job.
    • Increased health of slither.io boss (240 -> 300)
      • Developer's note: slither.io could be burst down too easily, especially for a tier 2 boss.
    • Increased starting health hultiplier for easy and medium difficulties (1.5x -> 1.8x for easy, 1.2x -> 1.4x for medium)
    • Decreased enemy spawn rate for easy and medium difficulties.
      • Developer's note: Since there are now multiple characters with different amounts of starting health, it has become a multiplier instead of a flat increase. Easy and medium mode were still too difficult so they get nerfed yet again.
    • Made every boss enemy deal knockback to the player instead of instantly killing them on contact
      • Developer's note: Instakilling the player on contact caused a lot of runs to suddenly die out, and it's always really frustrating when that happens. Now every boss deals knockback and a little bit of damage instead. All the bosses currently in the game either already deal knockback or rely on their projectiles for damage, so this shouldn't be too big of a nerf.
    • Made health potions survive longer before disappearing (10s -> 16.67s)

Update 1.5 - July 3, 2026

  • Added 1 new tier 3 enemy
  • Added 1 new upgrade
  • Made intro screen look slightly better
  • Added background color
  • Balance Changes:
    • Increased health of furnace zombie (15 -> 20)
    • Reworked speed scaling for furnace zombie (Before: 6-health/3. After: 6.5-health/4)
      • Developer's note: Furnace zombie felt way too underpowered for a tier 3 enemy.
    • Increased the rate at which health potions spawn (16.67s on average -> 10s)
    • Increased the range of healing amounts that health potions can give (1-4 -> 2-5)
      • Developer's note: Health potions felt too rare and not useful enough, leading to players being forced to pick healing-related upgrades in order to stay alive.
    • Decreased amount of times the 2x Exp Gain upgrade could be obtained (2 -> 1)
    • Nerfed wave 9 (2 tier 3 enemies -> 1 tier 3 enemy)
    • Nerfed wave 10 (3 tier 3 enemies -> 2 tier 3 enemies)
      • Developer's note: Waves 9 and 10 were still too difficult even on easy difficulty.
